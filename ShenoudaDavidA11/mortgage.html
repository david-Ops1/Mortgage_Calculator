Financial Calculator

Explanation

Enter the amount of the mortgage, change the yearly interest rates in percentage if you are quoted a different rate, and select Length of loan in number of years. Click the calculate button to view the monthly payment. Click the reload button to clear the form. For personal and business loans there will be a $1000.00 and $500.00 origination fees that will be included in monthly payments. There is no origination fees for an education loan.
